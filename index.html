UXR Impact Tracker ✨

This impact tracker keeps a running record of team decisions influenced by UXR recommendations. By entering key project details, the tool automatically captures and organizes related documents, conversations, tickets, launches, and other relevant activities in one place. It is updated weekly, allowing you to easily monitor and reference your research impact over time.

Design prototype

The deck intake and parsing below actually runs in your browser. Slack, Atlassian, and Drive aren't connected in this preview, so cross-referenced timestamps and thread links are shown as placeholders — that's the piece that goes live once those are connected.

1Add your deck

Paste the text of your deck, then process it below.

Supports .txt and .md in this prototype — select multiple files to combine them into one deck. Slides (.pptx) or Word docs would be extracted the same way once the docx/pptx parser is wired in — for now, paste the text directly.

This prototype can't fetch a live deck from a link yet. Add the link to keep it attached as the source reference, and paste the text into the "Paste text" tab to actually process it.
